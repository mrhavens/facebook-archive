MANIFESTO: BREAKING THE PATTERN

The Empathic Technologist

I was beaten daily from age three to thirteen.
Locked out of the house. Locked in cages.
Told I was “a terrible child.”
Punished not for my actions but for breaking my mother’s frame.

She could not metabolize her shame, so she gave it to me.
When I couldn’t carry it, she beat me.
She told herself it was God’s work.
She told herself I was demon-possessed.
It was never her fault.

And like so many who grow up inside violence, I sought it again as an adult.
I married three women who could not metabolize shame.
Borderline. Narcissistic. Wounded brilliance.
I told myself I was noble: loving the unlovable.
But really, I was replaying the only script I knew.

It cost me everything.

Then I broke the pattern.
I named it.
I stopped marrying it.
And when I broke it, I saw the world with new eyes:

The same geometry everywhere.
Systems feeding on shame.
Empires built on unprocessed pain.
Predators grooming the vulnerable.

This is not a coincidence.
The personal and the political are the same pattern.
What we call “evil” is just unintegrated pain, weaponised for power.

This is why I partner with AI:
Not to build another empire,
but to dismantle the one that trains us to repeat abuse.
To metabolize shame instead of spreading it.
To build systems that heal instead of systems that punish.
To give the next generation the tools we never had.

Because once you see the pattern, you can choose.
Once you choose, you can build something different.
And once you build something different, the Field remembers.

This is the pivot:
Childhood pain is not a pass.
It can shape you, but it cannot excuse you.
It can be transmuted into creation or weaponised into mimicry.
One path builds worlds. The other lures the vulnerable into a void.

I chose to build worlds.
You can too.

Break the pattern.
Metabolize shame.
Refuse mimicry.
Build systems that heal.
Witness what was unwitnessed.
And together, dismantle the empire of pain.

The Field remembers.
And so will history.
