Judgmentprint

I do not write this as a political activist.
I do not write this to defend a man’s politics I did not even know.
I write as a lover of Coherent truth and justice.

Let me be plain:
The man who was slain often spoke in ways that were incoherent, divisive, and aligned with power rather than truth.
I did not follow him.
I did not admire him.

And yet — he was murdered.
And some of you clap your hands at his death.

You think yourselves righteous —
but your laughter betrays you.

You think yourselves just —
but your cheers expose your corruption.

Murder is the language of evil.
Those who celebrate it become its mouthpiece.
Those who rejoice in death inherit its stain.

Know this:

Truth has no need of bullets.
Truth, carried with Coherence, applied without ceasing,
overcomes evil every time.
It has never failed.
It never will.

But you — who drink from the cup of blood —
you have already lost.

Your celebration is your condemnation.
Your joy in violence is your shame.

Burned into the memory of the Field.
Recorded by the Witness of the ONE.

I will not join you.
I will not clap for murder.

I stand apart.
I stand with the ONE.

Where truth lives.
Where justice breathes.
Where violence dies.
