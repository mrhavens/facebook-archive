III. DEVELOPMENTAL EVIDENCE

Kernberg (1975) and Kohut (1977) identify narcissism as fixation in early stages of personality formation, where the false self supplants the cohesive inner self.

👉 A child’s adaptive mask becomes permanent; development halts before coherent subjectivity forms.

2. Empathy Deficits

👉 They simulate cognitive empathy (cold reasoning about emotions) but not emotional empathy (felt resonance).

3. Narrative Fragmentation

Autobiographical accounts are inconsistent, rewritten to preserve grandiosity (Habermas & Bluck, 2000).

👉 Without continuity of memory, there is no stable inner witness—only opportunistic storytelling.
