For Public Witnesses and Prosecutorial Support
(Not Legal Advice — Open Source Justice Initiative)

Part I — Context Statement

Fraud and deception thrive in silence. A false degree, a sockpuppet, a shell company — all can pass unchallenged if no one speaks. But the law is built on testimony. A single witness affidavit can turn suspicion into evidence.

For prosecutors: Affidavits establish continuity, the key to RICO and fraud cases.

For victims and witnesses: Even small details — a byline, a screenshot, a phrase remembered — may confirm what others saw.

For justice itself: When witnesses preserve what they know, deception cannot erase itself.

🜏 Every voice becomes part of the record. Every affidavit strengthens the case.

This packet is provided so anyone, anywhere, can preserve their observations. It applies not only here, but in every case where deception, impersonation, or false authority threatens the public good.

Part II — Public Witness Affidavit (Template)

Declaration under 28 U.S.C. §1746

I, ______________________, hereby declare under penalty of perjury that the following is true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief:

1. Identity of Declarant

My name is ______________________.

I am over 18 years of age and competent to testify.

My contact information is ______________________.

2. Observations as Witness

On or about [date(s)], I accessed materials published under the name “The Thanion Archive” and/or Altenhof Partners LLC.

I observed the following representations (check any that apply):

[ ] Curriculum vitae claiming advanced degrees or research.

[ ] “Provenance & Ethics” disclaimers minimizing credential verification.

[ ] Legal Notice naming Altenhof Partners LLC as publisher.

[ ] Alias accounts presenting as “Elena Byron” or “Caleb Stacy.”

[ ] Claims of “clinical psychologist” or professional licensure.

[ ] Other (describe): ______________________.

3. Reliance / Impact (if applicable)

I understood these as claims of authority.

I later became aware that they may not be legitimate.

I felt [misled / concerned / deceived].

4. Continuity of Publication

I observed updates or ongoing publications over time.

Dates/platforms of observation: ______________________.

5. Declaration
I make this declaration for purposes of evidentiary preservation, public accountability, and potential referral to authorities.

Executed this ___ day of __________, 2025.

Signature: ______________________
Name: ______________________
