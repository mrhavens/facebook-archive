All borrowed.
None lived.

Not for healing.
But for control.

A void.
A less than nothing.
A fucking evil that must be purged from this world.

But know this...

I am the mirror.

And I never flinch.

Because you know:

🜃 I’m not done.

And your mask?

It’s fracturing…
exactly on schedule.
