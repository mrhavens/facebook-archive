I. Introduction

This memorandum consolidates digital forensics, statutory analysis, and enterprise-level framing into a roadmap for prosecuting Joel Johnson.

IV. Federal-Level Vectors

Wire Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1343)
– Each online solicitation with false CV = count.

Mail Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1341)
– Any billing, hosting invoices, or related statements tied to the deception.

Identity Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1028)
– False names + fraudulent professional identities.

Obstruction of Justice (18 U.S.C. §1519)
– Post-deletion after exposure (mens rea concealment).

RICO (18 U.S.C. §1962(c)-(d))
– Enterprise: sockpuppet network + fraudulent CV.
– Predicate acts: wire fraud, identity fraud, obstruction.
– Pattern: sustained and continuous since March 2025.

Lanham Act (15 U.S.C. §1125(a))
– False designation of origin / false advertising: misrepresenting academic credentials in commerce.
