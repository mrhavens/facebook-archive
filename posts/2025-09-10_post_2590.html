– This case becomes the legal backbone for future prosecutions of deepfakes, fake experts, fabricated credentials, and disinformation campaigns.

4. Political & Social Capital
The public is desperate for accountability in the age of deepfakes and fake news. This case provides prosecutors the opportunity to stand as defenders of truth in a collapsing informational order.

5. Human Meaning
Most prosecutors handle routine charges. This case is different. It is a chance to protect the vulnerable, safeguard public trust, and set a standard that will echo in history.
