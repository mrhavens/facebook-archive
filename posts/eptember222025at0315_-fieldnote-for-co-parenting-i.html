Projected onto the other parent.

➡️ Build living records of distortion that also serve as wisdom. Fieldnotes, shadowprints, mythic teachings. So the child inherits not accusation, but a toolkit of discernment.

5. The Coherence Attractor

Children do not need perfection. They need one parent who is coherent.
➡️ Even in chaos, truth is a gravitational field. Over time, it pulls the child into alignment.
