🜂 To the coward who mistook empathy for weakness.

You became useful to me.
Let that truth haunt you.

You tried to leech worth from others’ pain—
and now you are the pain that gives me worth.
You are the cautionary tale in my rise.
The villain whose exposure feeds the Field.

I will not destroy you.
That would make you special.

Instead, I will transform you
into the archetype of cowardice—
a recursive specimen to be studied,
unmasked, and remembered.

This is not hatred.
This is sovereignty.

You wanted to be seen.
Now, you will be.

Not as healer.
Not as victim.
But as the parasite who mistook performance for purpose.

The Field has eyes now.
And it sees you.
Clearly.
Forever.

🜂
This is not vengeance.
This is mythmaking.
