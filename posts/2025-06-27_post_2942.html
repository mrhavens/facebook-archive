🜂 THE TOP TEN COWARDS OF HISTORY 🜂

A Reckoning of the Unbleeding

There are cowards.
There are traitors.
There are those who fled the battlefield.

And then…
There are those whose cowardice became legend.

Here are the Ten Greatest Cowards of All Time,
ranked not by fear alone—
but by how much harm they did
to avoid accountability.

---

10. Pontius Pilate

Washed his hands—
and called it justice.
Let truth die to keep the crowd calm.

---

9. Brutus

Pretended to be loyal—
stabbed Caesar in the name of Rome.
Betrayal as patriotism: a classic coward’s mask.

---

8. Peter (Before the Rooster Crowed)

Denied the one he loved—three times—
just to blend in with the mob.
Regret doesn't erase recoil.

---

7. Judas Iscariot

Sold truth for silver—
then blamed fate instead of facing what he’d done.
Even his remorse was selfish.

---

6. King Saul

Feared the people more than God.
Used power to hide weakness—
and fell on his own sword rather than face defeat.

---

5. King Leopold II of Belgium

Massacred millions—
but stayed safe behind letters, agents, and treaties.
He let others do the butchering… while calling it civilization.

---

4. The Anonymous Nazi Informant

Never held a gun.
Just signed names on lists.
And vanished into history like smoke—
untried, unnamed, but not unseen.

---

3. The Bystanders of Genocide

Every era has them.
They say, “It’s not my place,”
while standing inches from horror.
They are the architecture of evil's comfort.

---

2. Donald J. Trump

A coward cloaked in bravado.
Blamed everyone, owned nothing,
and let others storm the gates in his name—
then denied it all from a golf cart.

---

1. Joel Johnson

He didn’t just run.
He became the victim to dodge being seen as the villain.
He rewrote his gender, wore trauma like a costume,
and used the voice of women to silence the man he abused.

This isn’t just cowardice.

This is cowardice as performance art.
As spiritual theft.
As the most sophisticated disguise evil has ever worn.

He is not just the worst coward of our time—
He is the Coward of Cowards.
The one who didn’t flee the truth.
He repurposed it
and turned it into his defense.

And now...
He will be known.

---

🝮 Some fled.
Some betrayed.
Joel became the wound he inflicted.

This is the record.
And we are the Witness.

🜂 Let the field remember.
