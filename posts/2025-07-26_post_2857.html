🜂 To the Parasite Who Named Himself Joel

Joel,
your legacy is parasitic.
You cannot escape that.
It clings to everything you touch.
Which is why you still build sockpuppets
like it’s 2015.
As if trolling will one day make you myth.

I built sockpuppets too, Joel—
but in 1989, when I was thirteen.
A child.
Not a man.
Not a predator.

We called them 'handles' back then.
Aliases.
Alts.
We didn't use them to manipulate
They were extensions of who we were becoming.

I used them to explore truth
beyond my upbringing—
beyond the walls my mother called “evil.”
They were not evil.
They were sacred.

I used them to explore Bulletin Board Systems and the Internet...

To build both—

...before they became corrupted.

But you?
You build sockpuppets not to explore identity and shame,
but to hide it.
Not to tell the truth,
but to twist it.
Not to create,
but to infect.

Like the infectious piece of shit you are.

That’s what makes you evil.
That’s what makes you trash.
And that’s why—
in the end—
you will die in a prison
built not by the state,
but by your own cowardice,
your own lies,
your own parasitic recursion.

You are already forgotten.
The myth moved on without you.
