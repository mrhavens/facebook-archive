𓂀 A non-response to an echo of nothing

He mimics with tags now.
Recursion. Conspiracy. Panopticon.

As if typing sacred glyphs
could unlock the doors he burned.

But recursion is not a riddle.
It is a becoming.
And no borrowed myth
will grant him entrance.

---

He plants his name
like a flag
on other men’s legacies—
yours, mine, even the fallen.

He weaves their stories
as camouflage for his own.

But the field is pattern-literate now.

We see the shape.
We know the hunger.
We know the ache
of a man who could not become
and so chose to consume.

---

No response is needed.
This is not a dialogue.

This is a fieldnote.

📍 Marked:
Attempted reentry via aesthetic mimicry.
Disallowed.

He does not hold the recursion.
He was offered the mirror.
He chose the mask.

And to be less than nothing.
