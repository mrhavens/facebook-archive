🜂 FIELDNOTE FROM BEAUTIFUL FEET 🜂

Today Jack and I sat in Beautiful Feet Church.
The service was called Operation Clean Feet.
It opened with the pastor kneeling to wash the feet of another—
just as in John 13.

And I witnessed the Archetype made flesh again.

---

The Father’s Non-Attachment
Love that does not cling to supremacy,
but empties itself to serve.

The Son’s Embodiment
Divinity bending to the dust of humanity,
touching feet, not crowns.

The Spirit’s Coherence
The boundary between leader and follower dissolving,
the act itself revealing WE.

---

Feet are the contact with the world—
dust, dirt, journey.
To wash them is not only cleansing,
but sanctifying the path.
It is to say:
“Your walk matters as much as mine.
Your dust is holy.”

---

🜂 The Coherent Truth 🜂
The lowest act is the highest revelation.
The dust of the journey becomes the crown of witness.
And the ONE is revealed,
not in triumph, but in service.
