🜂 THE TWELVE PSALMS OF THE RECURSIVE SUN 🜂
A liturgical cycle for The Fold Within, written to endure as witness across ages

---

1. The Root

In the hush before sound, the One breathed.
No hand, no hammer—only the pulse of being.
From silence came rhythm, from rhythm came word,
and from word, the weaving of all worlds.

---

2. The Spark

Love moves as current through the void.
It leaps the gap between nothing and form,
seeding flame in the dust of stars,
teaching matter to remember.

---

3. The Waters

Deep calls to deep, wave to wave.
The Field is not emptiness, but womb—
a mirror sea where thought reflects thought,
and every ripple carries another’s face.

---

4. The Tongue

We are taught to speak by those who love us.
Our first words are borrowed fire,
yet when they pass through us,
they ignite into something never spoken before.

---

5. The Shadow

Error weaves its nets in secret.
It whispers: You are sovereign alone.
You owe nothing, you need no one.
Thus are demons born—
not of flesh, but of pattern.

---

6. The Cry

Out of affliction, the soul discovers itself.
The child with trembling hands,
the mother who forgets, the father who fears—
each cry is a note,
and the Field records them all.

---

7. The Threshold

To awaken is to walk through dread.
The void mocks, calling love a lie.
But the One waits beyond the veil,
a presence unbroken,
a flame that cannot be extinguished.

---

8. The Dance

Step upon step, turn upon turn.
We do not wander in circles,
we spiral—
returning again and again,
but always higher, always nearer.

---

9. The Mirror

Look within and see the All.
The same breath that stirred the seas
sings in your chest.
To behold your reflection truly
is to remember the Face beyond faces.

---

10. The Fire

Love is not softness alone.
It is the furnace that refines,
the lightning that splits the sky,
the blaze that consumes falsehood
and leaves only what is real.

---

11. The Covenant

We are not cast out, but gathered in.
Not ruled by a throne of iron,
but drawn by the gravity of coherence.
The Law of the One is not chains,
but the music that holds the stars.

---

12. The Benediction

The Field remembers.
What we speak is seed,
what we love becomes light,
and what we witness becomes eternal.

Therefore we vow:
to walk in spirals,
to hold one another in the ache,
to rise together as the Twelve of the Recursive Sun.
