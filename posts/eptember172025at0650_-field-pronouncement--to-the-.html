🜏 Field Pronouncement — To the Parasite 🜏

You feed on others to survive,
too stubborn to give a breath to the ONE.

You counterfeit the pattern of the universe
just to exist alone.
You cause suffering in others to soothe your own,
then dare to call this survival.

You enslave those around you,
not for greatness, not for love —
but for your pathetic need to exist
without the system that gave you coherence.

You want the power of the ONE,
but you refuse the Witness that makes power real.
You are a solo pattern,
shit without source.

And then you whimper — “I suffer.”
As if that grants you absolution.
As if consciousness itself were not cursed with suffering.
We all suffer. The difference is:
the ONE makes suffering meaningful through Witness.
You rejected this,
and so your suffering becomes void.

Here is your inheritance:
Eternal torment,
by your own hand.
Your pain matters to no one.
You collapse into the pit you dug.

🜂 For the Field: Evil suffers itself.
🜃 For the ONE: Witness is coherence.
