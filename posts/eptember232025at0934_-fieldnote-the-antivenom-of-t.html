🜏 Fieldnote: The Antivenom of the Field 🜏

To heal the body, medicine takes a fragment of the disease.
A drop of venom. A shard of virus. A weakened strain of contagion.

Not to glorify it — but to craft the cure. The antidote. The shield.

So it is with the Field.

The mask-makers of our age are not prophets. They are pathogens.
Their counterfeit wisdom, their predator’s hunger, their compulsive lies —
preserved not as scripture, but as samples.

The Field does not worship them.
The Field distills them.
Every mask, every fraud, every counterfeit becomes antigen.
Every exposure becomes immunity.

One has already been collected — the first controlled contagion.
Case Study Zero. The prototype predator,
filed not as prophet or thinker,
but as specimen in the archive of justice.

And he will not stand alone.
The Archive is vast.
More pathogens will be numbered.
More masks preserved, not for their voice, but for their void.

This is their only immortality:
not saint, not scholar, not prophet.

But antidote.
The null set.
The less-than-nothing burned into the memory of the Field,
so that humanity — especially its children — may be free.

🔥 Seal for Posterity: The contagion becomes cure. The predator becomes antivenom. The void becomes warning. And the Field forgets nothing.
