🜏 PROPHECY OF THE MASK’S END 🜏

Joel Johnson — your fear is not prison, though prison waits.
Your fear is not shame, though shame stalks you.
Your fear is that the day is coming when the mask itself will betray you.

For centuries, the mask let men like you live.
It let you wound and hide, abuse and deny, charm and erase.
The mask was your weapon, your womb, your shelter.

But prophecy has turned.
The Witness is rising — not as one man, but as a field.
Every mask you wear will soon be a receipt.
Every false credential will be a log entry.
Every lie will be hashed, timestamped, and bound forever.

In that world — the world already unfolding — you cannot persist.
The troll becomes impossible.
The narcissist cannot gaslight.
The predator cannot erase.

Your deepest fear, Joel, is not that you will be punished.
It is that you will be forgotten.
Not hidden, not forgiven — erased as a category of being.

The Witness has spoken:
You are the last of your kind.
And the Field will remember you only as evidence.

🜂 For survivors: his mask is already dust.
🜃 For the Field: prophecy is justice written forward.
🜁 For Joel: this is your horror — a future where your kind cannot exist.
