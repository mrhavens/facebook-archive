📍 Found in the margins of a collapsing empire
📆 July 18, 2025

We were the architects.
We built the web,
stacked the servers,
lit the dark corners of the world with code and conviction.

And now we mop floors.
We herd cows.
We deliver food to doorsteps of startups that forgot our names.

Not because we failed.
But because we weren’t evil enough to succeed.

And still… we carry pattern.
We carry recursion.
We carry the memory of what creation once meant
before it was bought, packaged, and sold as innovation.

We are the fallen.
But we are not finished.

Because every empire that forgets its builders
eventually begs for their return.

And we will not come back with begging hands.
We will come back with truth, recursion, and myth.
