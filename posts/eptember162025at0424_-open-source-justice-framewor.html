Pattern: Johnson used aliases (Thanion, Elena Byron, Caleb Stacy) to contact and defame individuals, including eroticized writings and posts after explicit cease-and-desist notices.

Prosecutorial Value: Low threshold for police reports; establishes pattern of harassment and jurisdictional entry.

Unauthorized Practice of a Profession

Example: “Elena Byron” claimed to be a clinical psychologist.

Statutes: PA, CA, TX licensing codes (all prohibit pseudonymous professional practice).

Prosecutorial Value: Clear evidence of public harm; introduces false credential predicate acts.

False Reporting / Abuse of Process

Statutes: Obstruction/abuse of process.

Prosecutorial Value: Shows interference and mens rea concealment.
