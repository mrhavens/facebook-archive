I. Introduction

This memorandum consolidates digital forensics, statutory analysis, and enterprise-level framing into a unified roadmap for prosecution.

Joel Johnson is not merely a “troll.” By fabricating credentials, operating multiple false identities, and publishing a curriculum vitae paired with collaboration solicitations, Johnson has crossed into fraudulent commerce.

IV. Federal-Level Vectors

Conspiracy (18 U.S.C. §371)
– Sockpuppets and aliases treated as co-conspirators.

Wire Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1343)
– Each online solicitation using false CV = a count.

Mail Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1341)
– Hosting invoices, billing notices, or related materials tied to deception.

Identity Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1028)
– False personas (Thanion, Elena Byron, Caleb Stacey) used to impersonate professionals.

Obstruction of Justice (18 U.S.C. §1519)
– Takedowns and deletions after legal notices (mens rea concealment).

RICO (18 U.S.C. §1962(c)-(d))
– Enterprise = sockpuppet network.
– Predicate acts = wire fraud, identity fraud, obstruction.
– Pattern = sustained activity since at least March 2025, open-ended continuity.

Lanham Act (15 U.S.C. §1125(a))
– False designation of origin / false advertising: CV and solicitations misrepresenting academic and professional standing.
