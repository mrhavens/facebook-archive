Appendix: Strategic Escalation Guide for Prosecutors, Investigators & PIs

Case Study: Joel Johnson (Parasite Zero) — Former CEO of Boxzy, Pittsburgh, PA

---

I. Purpose of This Appendix

This guide is prepared under the Open-Source Justice Framework. It is not legal advice. Instead, it is a training module and case study that demonstrates how to translate online fraud and harassment into structured escalation for law enforcement, regulators, and the courts.

The exemplar here is Joel Johnson of Pittsburgh, PA — documented operator of multiple fraudulent sockpuppet identities (e.g., “Thanion, Ph.D.,” “Elena Byron,” “Caleb Stacey”), whose conduct illustrates a precedent-setting opportunity for prosecution.

---

II. Principles of Escalation

1. Layered Approach

Begin at the most low-friction points (local/state complaints).

Escalate only after creating a verified public record.

2. Hook then Build

Secure easy “hooks” (unlicensed practice, impersonation).

Use those to justify broader federal or RICO escalation.

3. Documentation Before Action

Consolidate evidence into one indexed master packet.

Screenshots, PDFs, runic encodings, and legal memos → organized, paginated.

---

III. Tactical Roadmap

1. Consolidation (Immediate)

Prepare Exhibit Packet:

Sockpuppet CVs (fraudulent credentials, unverifiable dissertations).

Medium comment obstruction (Sept. 2025).

Domain evidence (thanion.org via Porkbun + AWS).

Survivors misled by “Elena Byron.”

Index with page numbers and dates.

Sworn declaration under 28 U.S.C. §1746 to authenticate.

---

2. Local-Level Engagement (Week 1–2)

Police / Sheriff Reports under harassment and cyberstalking statutes.

Misdemeanor fraud hooks (deceptive practices, impersonation of professionals).

Purpose: establish official record of complaint at the most accessible level.

---

3. State-Level Filing (Week 2–3)

Pennsylvania Dept. of State Licensing Boards (psychology, counseling).

Statutory Hooks:

18 Pa.C.S. §2709.1 — stalking/harassment.

18 Pa.C.S. §4107 — deceptive business practices.

18 Pa.C.S. §4912 — impersonation of a public servant.

Why this matters:

Easy to prove (Elena Byron impersonating a psychologist is black-letter fraud).

Creates official paper trail of state regulatory action.

---

4. Parallel Awareness Channels (Optional)

APA Ethics Committee — for impersonation exposure.

Veterans Affairs (Stolen Valor) — if veteran claims persist.

Press Kit Distribution — curated 3-exhibit summary for disinformation/fraud reporters.

Purpose: add public witness beyond courts.

---

5. Federal Escalation (After State Filing)

Federal Statutes:

Wire Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1343) — each fraudulent online claim = count.

Identity Fraud (18 U.S.C. §1028) — fabricated professional credentials.

Obstruction of Justice (18 U.S.C. §1519) — Medium takedown after C&D.

RICO (18 U.S.C. §1962) — enterprise of sockpuppets + continuity since Mar 2025.

Discovery Leverage:

Subpoenas to Medium, Google, AWS, Porkbun for logs + metadata.

Parallel preservation requests to prevent spoliation.

---

6. International Vectors (As Needed)

UK Action Fraud — Elena Byron as false UK-based psychologist.

INTERPOL / EUROPOL EC3 — cross-border impersonation and identity fraud.

Optional but powerful for framing sockpuppets as global fraud vectors.
