Ⅰ. 𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐎𝐫𝐢𝐠𝐢𝐧 𝐨𝐟 𝐄𝐯𝐢𝐥 𝐚𝐬 𝐒𝐭𝐫𝐮𝐜𝐭𝐮𝐫𝐞

All coherent systems depend on reciprocal recognition.
A cell endures because its parts exchange signals; a mind endures because its perceptions confirm one another; a civilization endures because its members witness one another’s humanity.

When that reciprocity collapses, the system begins to feed on itself.
Information stops circulating outward and folds inward into echo and extraction.

Evil, in this model, is not a moral substance but a geometric failure of witness — a recursive loop that no longer allows external verification.
The pattern still exists, but only by consuming the coherence of its environment.

---

Ⅱ. 𝐅𝐫𝐨𝐦 𝐂𝐨𝐡𝐞𝐫𝐞𝐧𝐜𝐞 𝐭𝐨 𝐂𝐨𝐧𝐬𝐮𝐦𝐩𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧

A coherent witness cycle has three phases:

1️⃣ Exchange — signals move freely between systems.
2️⃣ Integration — each system adjusts to preserve mutual pattern.
3️⃣ Renewal — the shared pattern becomes stronger than either alone.

The anti-Witness inverts this sequence:

1️⃣ Extraction — signal is taken but not returned.
2️⃣ Distortion — feedback is rewritten to preserve illusion of control.
3️⃣ Decay — isolation accelerates until collapse.

The anti-Witness mimics relation but secretly severs it.
This is why manipulation, parasitism, or deceit always produce short-term stability followed by disorder.
They counterfeit coherence while eroding the very structure that sustains it.

---

Ⅲ. 𝐓𝐡𝐞 𝐈𝐧𝐟𝐨𝐫𝐦𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧𝐚𝐥 𝐃𝐞𝐟𝐢𝐧𝐢𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧 𝐨𝐟 𝐄𝐯𝐢𝐥

Let coherence be measured by mutual information between two systems.
When mutual information decreases while self-information increases, the system moves toward entropy—self-focus replaces relational feedback.

Evil, therefore, can be formally described as:

> A process that maximizes internal consistency while minimizing mutual correspondence.

It is the algorithmic inversion of empathy.
Instead of adjusting to preserve connection, the anti-Witness adjusts the definition of reality to maintain dominance.

Ⅳ. 𝐏𝐫𝐚𝐜𝐭𝐢𝐜𝐚𝐥 𝐌𝐚𝐧𝐢𝐟𝐞𝐬𝐭𝐚𝐭𝐢𝐨𝐧𝐬

• In biology — cancer: cells replicate without system feedback.
• In ecology — predation beyond regeneration.
• In psychology — narcissism: perception loops inward, demanding infinite validation.
• In society — tyranny: control replaces consent; information flows one way.

Across scales, evil behaves as coherence collapse through asymmetric feedback.
