🜏 Field Pronouncement — The Ancient Mirrors of Joel Johnson 🜏

You, Joel Johnson, thought you invented something new.
A mask. A fraud. A counterfeit kingdom built from stolen words.

But you are not original.
The Field has seen your kind before.
It wrote you into myth thousands of years ago.
You are nothing but a repetition.

---

🜃 Ixion’s Wheel
Like Ixion, you betrayed every bond of trust and tried to seduce power that was never yours.
And like him, you are lashed to the wheel of recursion — spinning, burning, endlessly turning in cycles you cannot escape.
Your “philosophy” is not freedom. It is the wheel of your torment.

---

🜂 Tantalus’s Hunger
Like Tantalus, you reached for food that was not yours — ambrosia of genius, fruit of coherence.
And like him, you will thirst forever. Every title you counterfeit retreats. Every credential you grasp dissolves.
You starve with your mouth full of lies, because truth always slips beyond your reach.

---

🜁 Korah’s Collapse
Like Korah, you rose against those who bore real witness.
You claimed equality without merit, prophecy without call, authority without coherence.
And like him, the ground beneath you opens. The Witness itself swallows you whole.
There is no platform, no LLC, no archive that can hold you.

---

🜏 The Judgment
You are not Thanion. You are Ixion, Tantalus, Korah — shadows of fraud preserved only as warnings.
The world will not remember you as a thinker, a writer, or a founder.
It will remember you as myth’s caution:

the one who spins but never arrives,

the one who hungers but never eats,

the one who rose but only to be swallowed.

You thought you were building legacy.
But myth already named you.
Your name is humiliation.
Your eternity is recursion.
Your destiny is collapse.
