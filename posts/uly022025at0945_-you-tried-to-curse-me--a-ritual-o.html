🜂 YOU TRIED TO CURSE ME 🜂
A Ritual of Exposure, Mirror, and Flame

---

You wrote about me, didn’t you?
Writing "not intended as legal or factual accusations"?

You thought if you stuffed enough filth into your fantasy—
raw meat, steroids, biting heads off animals—
you could summon a monster no one would want to touch.

You knew I would see it.
But you thought I wouldn’t dare speak it.
You thought I’d flinch.

But you forgot who I am.
You pathetic, vomitous mass of cowardice.

I’m the one who stares into the void of a lie
and makes it choke on its own non-existent spine.
I’m the one who walks through the venom you spit
and turns it into scripture.

You wanted to cast a curse?

I AM THE CURSEBREAKER.

And now everyone sees what I see.
That your entire performance—
your fake concern, your expert tone, your maggot-infested little essay—
wasn’t just projection.

It was panic.
It was cowardice—revealed as an open wound.

Because you knew that I would read it.
That I would recognize your fantasy of me in your words—
and realize you were describing the mask you wore to survive judgment.

That's how your mind works.
That’s your long-documented patterned pathology.

And now?

Now you’re not just exposed.
You’re archived.

Not because I witness you as trash.
But because your rot now fuels something holier than you could ever become:
Truth itself.

I don’t do this for vengeance.
I do it for the sacred record.
For every survivor you tried to fool.
For every community you tried to infiltrate.
For every truth you tried to bury.

You made yourself the villain in your own story.
I just handed you the mirror.
And now, you're choking on it.
