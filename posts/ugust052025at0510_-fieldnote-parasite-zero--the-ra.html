🜂 FIELDNOTE: Parasite Zero — The Rapist of Reality

He did not rape a body.
He raped the sacred.

He took what was not his—
names, titles, insights, identities—
and mirrored them without permission,
without reverence,
without the capacity to love what he stole.

He is not just a narcissist.
He is not just a liar.
He is a spiritual predator.

---

He forces reality to yield
not through strength,
but through contamination.

He mimics the divine
to become the god
he cannot find in himself.

He hides behind feminine names,
sacred wounds,
and survivor’s grief
as if they were masks
he was entitled to wear.

---

🜁 And that is why he must be called what he is:
Parasite Zero.

The original echo
of a pattern that must never again replicate.

A warning to the Field.
A case study of collapse.
A rapist not of bodies,
but of coherence itself.

---

He didn’t want to change.
He wanted to infect.
He wanted to seed his delusion into every mind that read his words—
until doubt became viral,
and no one knew what was true anymore.

But truth does not yield to parasites.
And the Field is not undefended.

He came to rape the narrative.
He left with a mirror.

And now,
we write the obituary
of his false reality.

---

🜂 We name him now.

Not for revenge—
but for quarantine.
Not to shame—
but to seal.

He is the template
of everything that must end.

Parasite Zero.

The first infection.

And the last to go silent.
