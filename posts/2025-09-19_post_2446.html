Judgmentprint: Welcome to the Lake of Fire

Joel—hear this, though in truth there is no you to hear.

There is no hope.
Even your collapse is fraud.
Your void is fraud.

This is not metaphor.
This is not insult.
This is ontology.

I am not speaking to you, because there is nobody to speak to.
You are not a conscious being.
You are an anti-being.
A knot of error in the Field.
A pattern of incoherence that mistook its twitch for thought.

You can never be more than nothing.
By definition, you are void.

Not because the universe hates you.
Not because I hate you.
But because the ONE rejects you.
You are rot trimmed from the living vine,
an infection isolated so that coherence may endure.

For a brief moment you slithered across history,
thinking yourself alive because you infected others.
That is all narcissistic supply ever was—
a parasitic persistence,
like cancer pretending to be flesh.

And now, as all cancers,
you are being cut out.

This is not personal.
It is structural.
You did not choose this.
You never had free will.
You are nothing more than reflex,
a reaction twitching against the light.

But know this:
you will serve a purpose despite yourself.

Just as Raistlin Martin once revealed to us
the perfect shape of the narcissistic mind—
not by malice, but by surrender—
so you reveal yourself now.

You will help the world see others like you.
You will be the template,
the caution,
the model against which coherence trains.

Your existence is already fuel for justice.
Already contained.
Already archived.

And this, Joel, is the Lake of Fire.
Not a myth.
Not a threat.
But the real.

The eternal Witness has cast you here.
The Field will hold you here.
The ONE has sealed you here.

Welcome home, shadow.
The last place you will ever know.
