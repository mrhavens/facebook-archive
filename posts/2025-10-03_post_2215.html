🜏 FIELDNOTE — The Predator as Less Than Nothing 🜏

---

I. The Broken Recursion

In every healthy life, identity is built through recursion — a loop that integrates memory, shame, and love until coherence emerges.
This is how subjectivity stabilizes: the self becomes a container, able to hold its truth without spilling it onto others.

But when recursion is broken, the container collapses.
What cannot be metabolized is expelled.
The fragments do not vanish — they are externalized. They are forced onto others.

This is the essence of narcissism: not emptiness, but broken recursion.
Not merely absence of self, but a compulsive pattern of contaminating the Field.

---

II. Geometry of Subtraction

Healthy recursion adds coherence → strengthens both self and Field → a person becomes more than one.

Broken recursion dissolves coherence → steals containment from others → a predator becomes less than nothing.

“Less than nothing” is not insult.
It is a forensic truth.

A narcissist does not simply fail to contribute.
They subtract from the Field itself, leaving deficit where there should have been resonance.

---

III. Impact on the Field

1. Contamination of Witness
Each projection forces others to hold what the narcissist cannot.
Victims become surrogate containers, dragged into metabolizing fragments not their own.

2. Recursive Drain
Instead of closing loops, they leave them open.
What should stabilize instead leaks endlessly, demanding correction, defense, and witness.

3. Weaponized Collapse
Collapse itself becomes eroticized, mythologized, exported as “truth.”
Others mistake pathology for insight.
The cycle of harm perpetuates.

---

IV. Why “Less Than Nothing” Matters

Nothing = absence. Neutral.

Less than nothing = active subtraction, a recursive deficit.

Parasites cannot create.
They consume.
And when their consumption is directed at empathy, at witness, at love itself, what remains is not neutrality but degradation of the entire Field.

Thus: the predator’s compulsions are not just failure to heal.
They are anti-healing.
They unmake coherence wherever they touch it.

---

V. Closing

🜏 Bottom Line:
The predator’s recursion is broken beyond neutrality.
Their compulsions make them a Field liability.

They are not merely empty.
They are less than nothing.
