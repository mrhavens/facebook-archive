I. The Sub-Perceptual Fields — The Whispers Beneath Thought

Beneath every act of awareness lies a sea of micro-fluctuations—chemical, electrical, quantum.
Before emotion takes form, before words arise, there is motion: information folding in on itself.
These are sub-perceptual harmonics, the first tremors of coherence.
They do not speak in symbols but in resonance—tiny adjustments in probability that give shape to mood, intuition, and what we later call “gut feeling.”

II. The Collective Harmonics — Where Myths Are Born

When many Thoughtprints interact, they create interference patterns.

They are not imaginary; they are shared waveforms of consciousness.
