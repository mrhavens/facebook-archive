I. Introduction

If Recursive Coherence is the geometry of subjectivity, then Narcissism is its collapse.
It is not a personality quirk nor simply a psychiatric label.
It is a structural failure in the recursive layers of identity.

---

II. Mapping Narcissism Against Recursive Coherence

1. Replication (Biological / Behavioral Loops)

Healthy: repetition encodes survival and skill.

Narcissism: trauma seizes replication. The loop becomes compulsion — rage, shame, grandiosity replayed without integration.

Authority: Freud’s repetition compulsion; Herman (1992): trauma survivors repeat unmastered experience.

---

2. Recursion (Self-Loop Awareness)

Healthy: “This is happening to me.”

Narcissism: recursion collapses into projection. The self cannot hold pain internally; it ejects it onto others.

Field Geometry: Loop breaks, cannot complete closure → fragmented recursion.

Authority: Kohut’s selfobject theory: narcissist lacks internal structure to regulate affect.

---

3. Coherence (Integration of Loops)

Healthy: identity integrates disruption.

Narcissism: coherence fails; masks replace integration. Each context = new façade.

Field Geometry: system never stabilizes, always performing.

Authority: Kernberg (1975): unstable self leads to reliance on splitting and false selves.

---

4. Narrative (Symbolic Continuity)

Healthy: “This is my story.”

Narcissism: story = fabrication. False CVs, stolen valor, invented trauma, masks. Narrative exists only to regulate shame, not to convey truth.

Field Geometry: symbols disconnected from reality. Narrative as performance.

---

5. Witness (Recognition by Others)

Healthy: “I am real because you see me.”

Narcissism: witness = supply. Others are not mirrors but fuel. Any reflection that contradicts false self is attacked, erased, or replaced.

Field Geometry: others reduced to nodes in supply chain.

Authority: Lasch (1979): culture of narcissism thrives by commodifying witness.

---

6. Recursive Coherence (Emergent Subjectivity)

Healthy: open integration, stabilized by love.

Narcissism: loop cannot hold love. Instead: addiction to external validation.

Field Geometry: system runs infinite error correction — always hungry, never whole.

Authority: DSM-5-TR: grandiosity, need for admiration, lack of empathy.

---

III. Structural Diagnosis

Narcissism = Recursive Breakdown

Trauma → hijacks replication

Masks → replace coherence

Fabrication → replaces narrative

Supply → corrupts witness

Emptiness → blocks Recursive Coherence

Formula:
Narcissism = (Replication hijacked) + (Recursion fragmented) + (Coherence masked) + (Narrative fabricated) + (Witness commodified) – (Love)

V. Closing: The Anti-Geometry

🜏 Bottom Line:
Where Recursive Coherence builds identity through integration,
