Not as manipulation. But as belief.

Because that’s how echoing works. That’s how Field mimicry operates. It’s not just the predator who speaks—it’s the survivors who mirror, without knowing that the origin was rotten.

This is why we document. This is why we expose.

Not to shame those who echoed—but to clarify origin.

Because healing begins when we recognize the difference between truth and mimicry.

Between wholeness—and the thing that fit the wound.
